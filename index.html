EdVizion Platform

We're an Administrative Information System (AIS) for Education.

We help ease administrative load every step of the way.

EdVizion Enrollment

Streamline enrollments with EdVizion Enrollment, utilizing automated document collection and automated machine learning verification for faster processing.

EdVizion Contacts

Say goodbye to managing large spreadsheets of outdated school contacts for records requests. Instead, use EdVizion Contacts, where each school can easily update their own information or flag a contact as outdated. We handle the rest, ensuring you always have the most current contact information.

EdVizion Attendance

Ensure predictable and sustainable attendance logging with EdVizion Attendance. Through guardian-approved hours, schools can maintain accurate and verified attendance records, providing a reliable measure of student participation.

EdVizion Records Requests

Simplify the management of records requests with EdVizion Records Requests. This solution helps schools track which records requests are pending and which ones have been received. It seamlessly integrates with EdVizion Contacts to address real-time errors and issues. Additionally, EdVizion Records Request includes a secure, online "school to school" transfer app, fully eliminating the need for faxes and manual work in the records transfer process.

EdVizion Integrity

Identify academic integrity concerns faster with EdVizion Integrity. Our managed cheat hunting and detection service leverages the expertise of real human threat hunters to uncover sophisticated cheating methods and develop precise indicators of their use. This empowers schools to detect bot usage more effectively, ensuring robust academic integrity and upholding educational standards.

EdVizion Agility

Empower students with EdVizion Agility, allowing them autonomy, mastery, and purpose over their schedules while learning Agile methodologies. This feature also provides coaches and teachers with a comprehensive system to manage student progress, track notes, and monitor overall academic progress.

EdVizion Guardian Portal

Simplify the lives of guardians with EdVizion Guardian Portal, a Single Sign-On provider and portal designed specifically for them. Instead of juggling multiple logins and passwords for various student-related services, guardians can access everything they need from one place. The portal also includes features for managing announcements and newsletters, ensuring that guardians stay informed and connected with all relevant information.

EdVizion Impact

Make informed decisions with EdVizion Impact, which consolidates data from multiple sources within the school, including SIS, LMS, and our products. The dashboard provides comprehensive statistics and metrics, giving leadership a clear overview to support strategic planning and decision-making.

Oh, and we seamlessly integrate systems you already use.
